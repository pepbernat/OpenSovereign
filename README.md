OpenSovereign is a set of specifications, modules, and tools to develop the foundations of a **sovereign, open, and collaborative digital governance infrastructure**, focusing on:

- Verifiable and self-sovereign digital identity
- Voluntary and transparent citizenship
- Modular and anti-plutocratic governance
- Digital arbitration and justice
- Efficient public service treasury

This project offers **specifications, protocols, and reference implementations** for communities, experimental governments, DAOs, and identity projects to build interoperable services with digital sovereignty principles.

> OpenSovereign is designed to be the basis of a future evolutionary "digital state", but **starting as an open and collaborative infrastructure**, not as a political entity with immediate sovereign recognition.
