# Roadmap to Sovereignty

This roadmap outlines the evolutionary path from an open-source repository to a recognized sovereign digital state.

## Phase 1: The Sovereign Kernel (Current)

**Goal:** Define specifications and build the "Minimum Viable State" (MVS) infrastructure.

- [ ] **Definition:** Manifesto, Constitution v0, Governance Model.
- [ ] **Architecture:** Specs for Identity, Registry, and Voting modules.
- [ ] **Community:** Gather core developers and legal contributors.
- [ ] **Mockups:** "State Dashboard" interface design.

## Phase 2: The Digital Foundation

**Goal:** Operational governance and legal existence.

- [ ] **Legal Wrapper:** Register a DAO LLC or Foundation (e.g., Wyoming, Marshall Islands, Switzerland) to interface with the old world.
- [ ] **Genesis Citizenship:** Issue first DIDs and Citizenship credentials (non-transferable).
- [ ] **Treasury V1:** Multi-sig treasury for funding development.
- [ ] **Arbitration Alpha:** First dispute resolution using Kleros or internal court.

## Phase 3: Population & Economy

**Goal:** Create internal value and GDP.

- [ ] **Service Marketplace:** Citizens offering services to citizens (B2B, B2C).
- [ ] **Mutual Insurance:** Health/Income insurance pools owned by citizens.
- [ ] **Business Registry:** Companies incorporated strictly within OpenSovereign.
- [ ] **Mass Migration:** Onboarding verified users from other networks.

## Phase 4: Territorial Anchoring

**Goal:** Physical footprint.

- [ ] **The "Embassy" Strategy:** Renting/buying small sovereign-lite plots or co-working hubs.
- [ ] **Charter City Partnership:** Negotiation with ZEDEs or Special Economic Zones to apply OpenSovereign law in a physical zone.
- [ ] **Diplomatic Outreach:** First bilateral agreements (even if just trade/recognition) with small nations.

## Phase 5: Sovereignty

**Goal:** International Recognition.

- [ ] **Observer Status:** Seeking observer status in international organizations.
- [ ] **Passport Acceptance:** Bilateral travel treaties.
- [ ] **Full Recognition:** The long-term horizon (Montevideo Convention compliance).
